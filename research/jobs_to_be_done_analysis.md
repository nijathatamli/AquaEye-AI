# Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD) Analysis: AquaEye AI

This analysis dissects the core user motivation for **AquaEye AI** using the Christensen Jobs-To-Be-Done framework to understand the progress municipal operators and emergency commanders are trying to make.

---

## 1. The Progress State Map

| Metric | Before State (Status Quo) | After State (AquaEye AI Enabled) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Operational Visibility** | Blind to flood points until vehicles get stuck or citizens dial 112. | Instant visual telemetry map showing city-wide runoff as it begins. |
| **Decision Speed** | Dispatch is reactive, lagging, and relies on chaotic traffic reports. | Proactive detouring and emergency dispatch completed within minutes. |
| **Data Collection** | No clean, numerical hydrology history to help plan city drainage. | Continuous, automated database of street submersion logs by node. |

---

## 2. The Three Job Layers

### 🔧 Functional Jobs (Funksional Tapşırıqlar)
*   **Identify Flash Runoff Immediately**: Detect standing water accumulation on primary transit arteries within Baku (e.g., Neftçilər, Yusif Səfərov) within 60 seconds of pooling.
*   **Calculate Actionable Metrics**: Calculate exact water depth (cm) and flow vector speed (m/s) to determine road safety boundaries.
*   **Optimize Emergency Dispatch**: Route road maintenance units and FHN emergency vehicles along safe paths during active downpours.
*   **Notify Citizens Proactively**: Push real-time detour coordinates to civil routing APIs (Waze, BNA map) to prevent traffic standstills.

### ❤️ Emotional Jobs (Emosional Tapşırıqlar)
*   **Eliminate Crisis Blindness**: Transition from a state of chaos (handling hundreds of conflicting emergency calls) to a state of absolute control and situational awareness.
*   **Avoid Tragic Traps**: Relieve the heavy burden of operator guilt associated with delayed dispatches (e.g., passenger vehicles drowning in Sabunchu tunnels).
*   **Operational Confidence**: Feel secure that decisions are backed by hard video telemetry rather than subjective citizen reports.

### 👥 Social Jobs (Sosial Tapşırıqlar)
*   **Signal Smart City Competence**: Present the municipal agency as a proactive, modern, and data-driven organization aligned with international smart-city indicators.
*   **Demonstrate Inter-Agency Coordination**: Establish prestige and reliability when presenting operational data to higher administrative bodies (DİN, Cabinet of Ministers).
*   **Earn Public Trust**: Prove to Baku residents that the state is actively safeguarding them using cutting-edge, citizen-centric technology.

---

## 3. The Hiring Triggers
*   **Trigger 1 (Acute Pain)**: A sudden autumn cloudburst hits Baku, and within 10 minutes, the 112 hotline is flooded with calls from drivers stuck on flooded avenues that operators thought were clear.
*   **Trigger 2 (Budget Friction)**: City planners request funds to install physical ultrasonic sensors, but the financial audit rejects the CapEx request due to roadwork disruption and high maintenance costs.
*   **Trigger 3 (Habit Frustration)**: A dispatcher spends 20 minutes manually reviewing 30 different CCTV feeds one-by-one to check which underpasses are flooded.

---

## 4. Competing Alternatives

| Alternative | Functional Limitation | Emotional/Social Limitation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Status Quo (Citizen Reports / 112 Calls)** | Lagging by 10-30 minutes. Imprecise location reporting. | High panic, high friction, reactive operations. |
| **Physical IoT Level Sensors** | High CapEx. Clog-prone. Vandalism/sludge damage. Long install times. | Creates visual clutter in the city; hard to justify ongoing maintenance costs. |
| **Manual CCTV Monitoring** | Human operator fatigue. Impossible to watch 10,000 cameras simultaneously. | High mental fatigue and constant fear of missing a critical event. |

---

## 5. Success Criteria (In the User's Own Words)
*   *"I want to know an intersection is flooding before a car gets stuck there, not after."*
*   *"I need to see the water level without leaving my desk or sending a patrol unit out in a rainstorm."*
*   *"I want a clean system that automatically filters out all the normal cameras and only alerts me when a camera sees actual water building up."*
